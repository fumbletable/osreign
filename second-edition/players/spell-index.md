# Spell List (Index)

Spells are divided into 6 Tiers. Each Tier contains **Cleric, Magic-User, and Druid** lists.

---

## Tier 1
- **Cleric:** Bless, Command, Cure Light Wounds, Detect Evil, Detect Magic, Light, Protection from Evil, Purify Food & Drink
- **Magic-User:** Charm Person, Detect Magic, Floating Disc, Hold Portal, Light, Magic Missile, Protection from Evil, Read Languages, Read Magic, Shield, Sleep, Ventriloquism
- **Druid:** Animal Friendship, Cure Light Wounds, Detect Magic, Entangle, Faerie Fire, Speak with Animals

---

## Tier 2
- **Cleric:** Bless Water, Find Traps, Hold Person, Know Alignment, Resist Fire, Silence 15 ft Radius, Speak with Animals
- **Magic-User:** Continual Light, Darkness 15 ft Radius, Detect Evil, Detect Invisibility, ESP, Invisibility, Knock, Levitate, Locate Object, Mirror Image, Phantasmal Force, Web
- **Druid:** Barkskin, Flame Blade, Heat Metal, Obscuring Mist, Speak with Plants, Warp Wood

---

## Tier 3
- **Cleric:** Continual Light, Cure Disease, Locate Object, Prayer, Remove Curse, Speak with Dead, Striking
- **Magic-User:** Clairvoyance, Dispel Magic, Fireball, Fly, Haste, Hold Person, Infravision, Invisibility 10 ft Radius, Lightning Bolt, Protection from Evil (10 ft), Slow, Water Breathing
- **Druid:** Call Lightning, Plant Growth, Protection from Fire, Speak with Dead Animals, Water Walk, Wind Wall

---

## Tier 4
- **Cleric:** Create Food & Water, Cure Serious Wounds, Neutralize Poison, Protection from Evil (10 ft), Speak with Plants, Sticks to Snakes, Tongues
- **Magic-User:** Charm Monster, Confusion, Dimension Door, Growth of Plants, Hallucinatory Terrain, Massmorph, Monster Summoning I, Polymorph Other, Polymorph Self, Remove Curse, Wall of Fire, Wizard Eye
- **Druid:** Animal Growth, Call Woodland Beings, Dispel Magic, Hallucinatory Terrain, Ice Storm, Wall of Thorns

---

## Tier 5
- **Cleric:** Commune, Cure Critical Wounds, Dispel Evil, Insect Plague, Quest, Raise Dead, True Seeing
- **Magic-User:** Animate Dead, Cloudkill, Conjure Elemental, Contact Other Plane, Feeblemind, Hold Monster, Magic Jar, Passwall, Telekinesis, Teleport, Wall of Iron, Wall of Stone
- **Druid:** Animal Summoning II, Commune with Nature, Control Winds, Insect Plague, Transmute Rock to Mud, Wall of Fire (greater)

---

## Tier 6
- **Cleric:** Animate Object, Blade Barrier, Find the Path, Heal, Part Water, Stone Tell, Word of Recall
- **Magic-User:** Anti-Magic Shell, Control Weather, Death Spell, Disintegrate, Geas, Invisible Stalker, Legend Lore, Lower Water, Move Earth, Reincarnation, Stone to Flesh, Wall of Ice
- **Druid:** Anti-Animal Shell, Conjure Fire Elemental, Control Weather, Earthquake, Reincarnation, Transport via Plants
