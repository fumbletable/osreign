# Cleric Spells — Tier 3

## Continual Light
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Near (30 ft)
- **Duration:** Permanent until dispelled
- **Effect:** Object glows like daylight (Far radius). Can blind 1 creature if CON save (12) fails.
- **Boost:** Affect +1 object.

## Cure Disease
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Duration:** Instant
- **Effect:** Ends one disease or poison affliction.
- **Boost:** Heal 1d6 HP as well.

## Locate Object
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Self
- **Duration:** Concentration, 1 Turn
- **Effect:** Sense direction of familiar object within 60 ft. {Ritual}
- **Boost:** Range doubles.

## Prayer
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Self (party, Near)
- **Duration:** 1 Turn
- **Effect:** Allies gain +1 to attacks/saves; enemies suffer –1.
- **Boost:** Duration +1 Turn.

## Remove Curse
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Duration:** Instant
- **Effect:** Lifts 1 curse from creature or object.
- **Boost:** Affect +1 target.

## Speak with Dead
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Near (corpse)
- **Duration:** 3 questions
- **Effect:** Ask a corpse questions; answers limited by what it knew in life. {Ritual}
- **Boost:** +1 question.

## Striking
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Touch (weapon)
- **Duration:** 1 Turn
- **Effect:** Weapon becomes magical; +1 to hit/damage.
- **Boost:** Duration +1 Turn.

# Magic-User Spells — Tier 3

## Clairvoyance
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Near (30 ft)
- **Duration:** 1 Turn
- **Effect:** See or hear a known location within range, even through barriers. Cannot see and hear simultaneously.
- **Boost:** Duration +1 Turn.

## Dispel Magic
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Near (30 ft)
- **Duration:** Instant
- **Effect:** End 1 ongoing spell or magical effect.
- **Boost:** Affect +1 target effect.

## Fireball
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Far (120 ft)
- **Duration:** Instant
- **Effect:** 20-ft radius blast deals caster level × d6 fire damage (DEX save 12 for half).
- **Boost:** +1d6 damage.

## Fly
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Duration:** 1 Turn
- **Effect:** Target flies at normal speed.
- **Boost:** Duration +1 Turn.

## Haste
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Near (party)
- **Duration:** 1 Turn
- **Effect:** Allies gain +1 attack each round, doubled movement. Afterward, 1 Fatigue.
- **Boost:** Affect +1 ally.

## Hold Person
- Same as Cleric version.

## Infravision
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Duration:** 1 hour
- **Effect:** Target sees heat signatures up to 60 ft.
- **Boost:** Duration +1 hour.

## Invisibility, 10 ft Radius
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Self
- **Duration:** Until broken (attacking/casting ends)
- **Effect:** All creatures within 10 ft become invisible.
- **Boost:** Radius increases to 20 ft.

## Lightning Bolt
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Far (120 ft line, 60 ft long, 5 ft wide)
- **Duration:** Instant
- **Effect:** Deals caster level × d6 lightning damage (DEX save 12 for half).
- **Boost:** +1d6 damage.

## Protection from Evil, 10 ft Radius
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Self
- **Duration:** 1 Turn
- **Effect:** Allies within 10 ft gain +1 AC/saves vs evil creatures.
- **Boost:** Radius expands to 15 ft.

## Slow
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Near (30 ft)
- **Duration:** 1 Turn
- **Effect:** Up to 6 creatures move at half speed, only 1 attack per round. WIS save (12) negates.
- **Boost:** Affect +1 target.

## Water Breathing
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Duration:** 1 hour
- **Effect:** Target breathes underwater.
- **Boost:** Affect +1 creature.

# Druid Spells — Tier 3

## Call Lightning
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Far (120 ft) outdoors only
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 Turn
- **Effect:** Summon 1 lightning bolt per round (2d6 damage, DEX save 12 half).
- **Boost:** +1d6 damage.

## Plant Growth
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Near (30 ft)
- **Duration:** Permanent
- **Effect:** Overgrowth creates difficult terrain, halving movement.
- **Boost:** Area doubles.

## Protection from Fire
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Duration:** 1 Turn
- **Effect:** Target immune to normal fire, half damage from magical fire.
- **Boost:** Affect +1 creature.

## Speak with Dead Animals
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Touch (corpse)
- **Duration:** 3 questions
- **Effect:** Ask a dead animal questions about what it sensed in life.
- **Boost:** +1 question.

## Water Walk
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Duration:** 1 Turn
- **Effect:** Up to 6 creatures can walk on water as solid ground.
- **Boost:** Duration +1 Turn.

## Wind Wall
- **Casting Time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Near (30 ft)
- **Duration:** 1 Turn
- **Effect:** 30-ft wall of wind deflects missiles, small flyers, and gas.
- **Boost:** Extend wall by 10 ft.
