## Armor

Armor protects you from harm but can restrict mobility.

| Armor           |   Cost | AC               | Properties                    | Min STR | Don/Doff       |
| --------------- | -----: | ---------------- | ----------------------------- | ------: | -------------- |
| None            |      – | 10 + DEX         | –                             |       – | –              |
| Leather (Light) |  20 gp | 12 + DEX         | Light                         |       – | 1 min          |
| Chain (Medium)  |  60 gp | 14 + DEX (max 2) | Medium                        |       9 | 5 min / 1 min  |
| Plate (Heavy)   | 300 gp | 16 + DEX (max 0) | Heavy, Bulky, Stealth SETBACK |      12 | 10 min / 5 min |
| Shield          |   7 gp | +1 AC            | Sundered (1 action to sunder) |       – | 1 action       |

**Rules**
- **Heavy armor:** causes Stealth SETBACK.
- **STR requirement:** if unmet, reduce Speed by 1 step (Normal → Slow).
- **Sleeping in Heavy armor:** CON save (Easy 8) or no rest benefits.
