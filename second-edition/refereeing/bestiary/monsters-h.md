# Monsters H

---

**Halfling** (HD 1) | XP 10
AC 12 | HP 4 | Mv Normal | Ml 8
Atk sword (d6) or sling (d4)
[Resist: poison, magic (saves at +2)]
Hide in wilderness (90%) or dungeons (2 in 6). Brave despite their size.
Small folk who prize comfort but make fierce defenders of hearth and home.

---

**Hawk, Giant** (HD 3) | XP 90
AC 13 | HP 12 | Mv Normal, Fly Fast | Ml 8
Atk 2 talons (d6), bite (d10)
[Darkvision]
Keen-eyed hunters. Can be trained if taken young. Some are intelligent and can speak.

---

**Harpy** (HD 3) | XP 90
AC 12 | HP 12 | Mv Normal, Fly Normal | Ml 7
Atk 2 claws (d3), club (d6)
Charming song (CHA 8 to resist, lures victims closer). Touch charms as Charm Person (CHA 8).
Human face, vulture body. Nests on cliffs and lures sailors to their doom.

---

**Hellhound** (HD 4-7) | XP varies
AC 15 | HP HD×4 | Mv Normal | Ml 9
Atk bite (d6)
[Darkvision]
Fire breath (HD d4s damage, Near, single target).
Flame-eyed hounds from the lower planes. Serve evil masters or hunt in infernal packs.

---

**Hippogriff** (HD 3) | XP 90
AC 14 | HP 12 | Mv Normal, Fly Fast | Ml 8
Atk beak (d10), 2 claws (d6)
Eagle head and wings, horse body. Easier to train than griffons, but less prestigious.

---

**Hobgoblin** (HD 1) | XP 10
AC 14 | HP 4 | Mv Normal | Ml 9
Atk sword (d8) or longbow (d8)
[Darkvision]
Larger, more disciplined goblins. Militaristic and cruel. Form organized warbands.

---

**Homunculus** (HD 2) | XP 40
AC 13 | HP 8 | Mv Slow, Fly Normal | Ml 7
Atk bite (d3)
[Darkvision]
Sleep bite (CON 8). Other variants: poison bite, detect magic/evil.
Tiny winged servant created by wizards. Abilities vary by creator's design.

---

**Horse, Riding** (HD 2) | XP 40
AC 12 | HP 8 | Mv Fast | Ml 6
Atk hooves (d4)
Standard mount. Skittish in combat unless trained.

---

**Horse, War** (HD 3) | XP 90
AC 12 | HP 12 | Mv Fast | Ml 8
Atk hooves (d8)
Trained for battle. Will kick and bite enemies. Expensive but invaluable.

---

**Human, Commoner** (HD 1/2) | XP 5
AC 10 | HP 3 | Mv Normal | Ml 6
Atk club (d4)
Farmers, merchants, craftsmen. The backbone of civilization and frequent victims of adventure.

---

**Hyena** (HD 2) | XP 40
AC 13 | HP 8 | Mv Fast | Ml 8
Atk bite (d6)
Scavengers and pack hunters of the savanna. Often kept as pets by gnolls. Clans can number up to 100.

---

**Hydra** (HD 5-12) | XP varies
AC 14 | HP HD×4 | Mv Normal, Swim Normal | Ml 10
Atk 1 bite per head (d6 each)
One head per HD. Great serpentine or lizard-like body with many necks.

*Some hydrae regrow heads. Some breathe fire. Referee's choice.*
