# Monsters D

---

## Dire Wolf
**HD 4 | AC 14 | HP 16**<br>
Atk bite (d8) | Mv Fast | Ml 8 | XP 160<br>
Larger, smarter wolves.<br>
Often found as mounts for goblins or in the service of darker powers.

---

## Displacer Beast
**HD 6 | AC 15 | HP 24** | Darkvision<br>
Atk 2 tentacles (2d4) | Mv Fast | Ml 8 | XP 360<br>
Displacement: Appears 3' from actual location. First attack against it always misses; subsequent attacks at SETBACK.<br>
Six-legged panther with two barbed tentacles. Hates blink dogs. Cruel and cunning.

---

## Djinni
**HD 7 | AC 14 | HP 28** | Darkvision<br>
Atk scimitar (2d8) | Mv Normal, Fly Fast | Ml 9 | XP 490<br>
Can create food, water, cloth, wood (metal fades). Illusions (vanish when touched). Gaseous form/Invisibility at will. Whirlwind form sweeps away 1 HD creatures.<br>
Air genies from the elemental planes. Cunning and proud. More powerful djinn grant wishes.

---

## Doppelganger
**HD 4 | AC 14 | HP 16** | Darkvision | Shapechanger | Immune: sleep, charm<br>
Atk claws (d12) | Mv Normal | Ml 9 | XP 160<br>
Perfect mimicry of any humanoid after brief observation. Reads surface thoughts.<br>
Infiltrators and assassins.

---

## Dragon
**HD 6-12 | AC 17+ | HP HD×4** | Darkvision | Immune: varies<br>
Atk 2 claws (d8), bite (3d6) | Mv Normal, Fly Fast | Ml 10 | XP varies<br>
Breath weapon (DEX DC by HD, damage = HD, shape varies by type). Scale HD and breath by age/size: Young (6-7), Adult (8-9), Ancient (10-12).<br>
Ancient, cunning, endlessly greedy. Each is unique in personality and power.

---

## Dryad
**HD 2 | AC 10 | HP 8** | Darkvision<br>
Atk wooden dagger (d4) | Mv Normal | Ml 6 | XP 40<br>
Powerful Charm (CHA 8 with SETBACK). Charmed victims rarely return—kept for a hundred years in the dryad's tree.<br>
Beautiful tree spirits who never venture far from their home trees.
