## Jackal
**HD 1/2 | AC 12 | HP 2** | Darkvision<br>
Atk bite (d4) | Mv Fast | Ml 7 | XP 5<br>
Harass: circle and nip at larger prey, never committing to a real fight.<br>

## Jackalwere
**HD 4 | AC 15 | HP 16** | Darkvision | Shapechanger | Immune: non-magical/non-silver weapons<br>
Atk bite (2d4) or weapon (d6) | Mv Normal | Ml 7 | XP 160<br>
Sleep Gaze (DC 8 WIS or sleep 4d4 turns). Can shift between jackal, human, and hybrid forms.<br>

## Jaguar

Atk 2 claws (d4), bite (2d4) | Mv Fast, Swim Normal | Ml 8 | XP 160<br>
