# Monsters C

---

## Camel
**HD 2 | AC 12 | HP 8**<br>
Atk bite (d4), spit (special) | Mv Normal | Ml 6 | XP 40<br>
Can travel for weeks without water. Spit causes no damage but is foul.<br>
Bad-tempered but essential in deserts.

---

## Carrion Crawler
**HD 3 | AC 12 | HP 12** | Darkvision<br>
Atk 8 tentacles (paralysis) | Mv Fast, Climb Normal | Ml 9 | XP 90<br>
Paralysing tentacles (DC 8 STR or paralyzed 2d4 turns).<br>
Feeds on carrion but attacks living prey. 10 ft long segmented horror.

---

## Cat, Mountain Lion
**HD 3 | AC 13 | HP 12**<br>
Atk 2 claws (d3), bite (d6) | Mv Fast | Ml 7 | XP 90<br>
Rear claw rake: If both fore claws hit, 2 additional claw attacks (d3 each).<br>
Ambush hunter. Stalks prey from above.

---

## Catoblepas
**HD 6 | AC 12 | HP 24** | Darkvision<br>
Atk bite (d6), tail (d8 + stun) | Mv Slow | Ml 8 | XP 360<br>
Death Gaze (DC 12 CON or die). Head so heavy it drags on the ground. Tail strike stuns for d6 rounds (no save).<br>
Buffalo-bodied beast with a grotesque head. Dwells in swamps.

---

## Cave Locust
**HD 2 | AC 14 | HP 8** | Darkvision<br>
Atk bite (d2), spit (special) | Mv Normal, Fly Normal | Ml 5 | XP 40<br>
Spit: brown goo causes nausea (DC 8 CON or -2 to all rolls for d6 turns). Shriek when startled attracts wandering monsters. Swarm Leap: when startled, all locusts leap randomly—everyone in Near range takes d4 damage and must save (DC 8 DEX) or be knocked prone.<br>
Swarms blacken cave ceilings. Disturb one and they all shriek.

---

## Centaur
**HD 4 | AC 12 | HP 16**<br>
Atk pike (d8), 2 hooves (d6) | Mv Fast | Ml 8 | XP 160<br>
Half man, half horse. Fierce warriors of myth—some evil, some aloof, some soothsayers.

---

## Chimera
**HD 9 | AC 15 | HP 36**<br>
Atk lion bite (2d4), goat horns (d4), dragon bite (3d4) | Mv Normal, Fly Fast | Ml 9 | XP 810<br>
Fire breath (DC 16 DEX for half, 3d8, Near cone, 3/day).<br>
Three-headed horror: goat, lion, and dragon. Great wings rise from its leonine body.

---

## Cockatrice
**HD 5 | AC 13 | HP 20**<br>
Atk bite (d6) | Mv Slow, Fly Normal | Ml 7 | XP 250<br>
Petrifying bite (DC 12 WIS or turned to stone).<br>
Bat-winged rooster with a serpentine tail. Struts with murderous intent.

---

## Crab, Giant
**HD 3 | AC 16 | HP 12** | Blindsight<br>
Atk 2 claws (2d6) | Mv Normal, Swim Normal | Ml 7 | XP 90<br>
Grip: if both claws hit same target, crab holds on—automatic 2d6 damage each round until target breaks free (DC 8 STR) or crab is killed.<br>
Lurks in tidal caves. Treats anything smaller than itself as food.

---

## Crocodile

Atk bite (3d4) | Mv Normal, Swim Normal | Ml 8 | XP 90<br>
Ambush: **Edge** on attack when striking from concealment in water.<br>
Explodes from still water. Victims rarely see it coming.

## Crocodile, Large

Atk bite (2d8) | Mv Normal, Swim Normal | Ml 9 | XP 360<br>

## Cyclops

Atk club (3d10) or rock (3d6, Far) | Mv Normal | Ml 9 | XP 1690<br>
