# Monsters P

**Pegasus** (HD 4) | XP 160
AC 10 | HP 16 | Mv Fast, Fly Fast | Ml 7
Atk 2 hooves (d8)
{Darkvision}
Winged horse. Most have feathered wings. Some might have bat wings and be evil.

---

**Phase Spider** (HD 2) | XP 40
AC 13 | HP 8 | Mv Normal, Climb Normal | Ml 7
Atk bite (d6 + poison)
{Darkvision}
Dimension Phasing: Shifts out of phase (cannot attack or be attacked), then returns to strike.
Lethal poison (CON 8 or drop to 0 HP).
Flickers between dimensions. Attacks from nowhere, then vanishes.

---

**Pixie** (HD 1/2) | XP 5
AC 14 | HP 2 | Mv Normal, Fly Fast | Ml 5
Atk dagger (d4)
{Invisible}
Naturally invisible - don't become visible when attacking. After 1 round, general location known (attacks at SETBACK).
Nasty, treacherous fey. More powerful variants have sleep arrows (attack with EDGE).

**Purple Worm** (HD 15) | XP 2250
AC 13 | HP 60 | Mv Fast, Burrow Normal | Ml 10
Atk bite (2d12), stinger (d8 + poison)
{Darkvision} {Tremorsense}
Swallow Whole (attack roll 4+ over needed): Swallows horse-sized or smaller prey.
Poison sting (CON 16 or drop to 0 HP).
Massive annelids 40+ ft long. Chew tunnels through solid rock. Desert variants are tan.
