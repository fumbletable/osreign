# Monsters P

---

## Pegasus
**HD 4 | AC 10 | HP 16** | Darkvision<br>
Atk 2 hooves (d8) | Mv Fast, Fly Fast | Ml 7 | XP 160<br>
Intelligent: understands speech, chooses its rider. Will not serve the cruel or cowardly.<br>
Winged horses prized above all other mounts. Cannot be bought—only earned.

---

## Phase Spider
**HD 2 | AC 13 | HP 8** | Darkvision<br>
Atk bite (d6 + poison) | Mv Normal, Climb Normal | Ml 7 | XP 40<br>
Dimension Phasing: Shifts out of phase (cannot attack or be attacked), then returns to strike. Lethal Poison (DC 8 CON or drop to 0 HP).<br>
Flickers between dimensions. Attacks from nowhere, then vanishes.

---

## Pixie
**HD 1/2 | AC 14 | HP 2** | Invisible<br>
Atk dagger (d4) or sleep arrow (Near) | Mv Normal, Fly Fast | Ml 5 | XP 5<br>
Naturally invisible—don't become visible when attacking. After 1 round, general location known (attacks at **Setback**). Sleep Arrow (1 in 10 pixies): DC 8 WIS or fall asleep as per Sleep spell (single target).<br>
Nasty, treacherous fey. The ones with tiny bows are the real threat.

---

## Purple Worm
**HD 15 | AC 13 | HP 60** | Darkvision | Tremorsense<br>
Atk bite (2d12), stinger (d8 + poison) | Mv Fast, Burrow Normal | Ml 10 | XP 2250<br>
Swallow Whole (attack roll 4+ over needed): Swallows horse-sized or smaller prey. Poison sting (DC 16 CON or drop to 0 HP).<br>
Massive annelids 40+ ft long. Chew tunnels through solid rock. Desert variants are tan.
