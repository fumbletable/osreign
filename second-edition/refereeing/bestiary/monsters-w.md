## Wasp, Giant
**HD 4 | AC 16 | HP 16** | Darkvision<br>
Atk sting (d4 + poison) | Mv Normal, Fly Fast | Ml 9 | XP 160<br>

Paper-nest builders. Aggressively defend their territory.

## Wight
