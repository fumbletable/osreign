# Monsters M

---

## Manticore
**HD 6 | AC 10 | HP 24** | Darkvision<br>
Atk bite (d8), 2 claws (d3), 1d6 tail spikes (d6 each, Near) | Mv Normal, Fly Fast | Ml 9 | XP 360<br>
24 iron tail spikes, regrow after long rest.<br>
Feral human face, lion body, bat wings, spiked tail. Speaks, but only to mock its prey.

## Mastodon
**HD 12 | AC 14 | HP 48**<br>
Atk gore (3d6), stomp (2d6) | Mv Fast | Ml 8 | XP 1440<br>
Trample: can move through smaller creatures, dealing stomp damage (DC 12 DEX to avoid).<br>
Huge shaggy elephants with curving tusks. Trained as war mounts by northern barbarians.

## Medusa
**HD 4 | AC 11 | HP 16** | Darkvision<br>
Atk bite (d10 + poison) | Mv Normal | Ml 8 | XP 160<br>
Petrifying Gaze (DC 8 WIS or stone in 1d4 rounds). Poison Bite (DC 8 CON or drop to 0 HP).<br>
Snake-haired woman with serpent's body below. Her lair is filled with statues.

## Merfolk
**HD 1 | AC 12 | HP 4** | Darkvision<br>
Atk spear (d6) or trident (d6) | Mv Normal, Swim Fast | Ml 7 | XP 10<br>
Breathes water. Can survive on land for about an hour before weakening.<br>
Human torso, fish tail. Build cities in shallow seas and coral reefs.

## Merfolk Chief
**HD 3 | AC 13 | HP 12** | Darkvision<br>
Atk trident (d8) | Mv Normal, Swim Fast | Ml 8 | XP 90<br>
Breathes water. Conch Horn: once per battle, summon 2d6 merfolk or 1d4 sharks.<br>
Rules from a throne of coral and bone. Commands loyalty through strength and lineage.

## Mimic

## Minotaur

## Mummy
**HD 5 | AC 10 | HP 20** | Darkvision | Immune: non-magical weapons (magic = half damage)<br>
Atk fist (d12) | Mv Slow | Ml 12 | XP 250<br>
Mummy Rot (DC 12 CON): prevents magical healing; wounds heal at 1/10 rate. Cure Disease = half rate. Remove Curse = full cure.<br>
