# Monsters E

---

## Eagle, Giant
**HD 4 | AC 12 | HP 16** | Darkvision<br>
Atk 2 talons (d4), bite (2d4) | Mv Normal, Fly Fast | Ml 8 | XP 160<br>
Snatch: On talon hit, DC 8 STR or grabbed. Drops victims for fall damage. Can also drop stones (2d6).<br>
Intelligent and proud. Speak their own tongue. Sometimes ally with those who respect the high places.

---

## Eel, Giant
**HD 5 | AC 13 | HP 20** | Blindsight<br>
Atk bite (3d6) | Mv Swim Fast | Ml 7 | XP 250<br>
Ambush: **Edge** when striking from concealment.<br>
Twelve feet of muscle and teeth, lurking in underwater caves. Patient hunters that strike from darkness.

---

## Efreet
**HD 10 | AC 17 | HP 40** | Darkvision | Immune: Fire<br>
Atk scimitar (3d8) | Mv Normal | Ml 10 | XP 1000<br>
Wall of Fire: DC 16 DEX or 2d6 fire. Wishes: Bound efreet grant 3 wishes (twist words maliciously).<br>
Fire genies from the City of Brass. Can be enslaved but scheme constantly for freedom. Cruel and proud.

---

## Elemental, Air
**HD 8/12/16 | AC 17 | HP HD×4** | Darkvision<br>
Atk slam (2d8) | Mv Fly Fast (Hover) | Ml 10 | XP varies<br>
Whirlwind: DC 12/16/16 STR or hurled (3d6 damage). Affects creatures of 1 HD or less.<br>
Living wind given terrible purpose. Barely intelligent but powerful as storms.

---

## Elemental, Earth
**HD 8/12/16 | AC 17 | HP HD×4** | Darkvision | Tremorsense<br>
Atk slam (4d8) | Mv Normal, Burrow Normal | Ml 10 | XP varies<br>
Grounded: **Setback** on damage vs airborne foes. Can tear down castle walls (1d4+4 rounds) or entire castles (1 hour).<br>
Hulking man-shapes of rock and soil. Silent, patient, and relentless.

---

## Elemental, Fire
**HD 8/12/16 | AC 17 | HP HD×4** | Darkvision | Immune: Fire<br>
Atk touch (4d8 fire) | Mv Fast | Ml 10 | XP varies<br>
Touch ignites flammables (item save per Referee).<br>
Formless masses of flame with vaguely human shape.

---

## Elemental, Water
**HD 8/12/16 | AC 17 | HP HD×4** | Darkvision<br>
Atk slam (3d10) | Mv Swim Fast | Ml 10 | XP varies<br>
Tethered: Cannot move more than Far from large water. **Setback** on damage vs foes not in water. Can overturn ships (1d4+4 rounds).<br>
Crashing wave given form and fury. Drags victims into the deep.

## Elephant
**HD 9 | AC 14 | HP 36**<br>
Atk 2 tusks (2d4), trample (4d8) | Mv Fast | Ml 8 | XP 810<br>
Trample: Charge attack on first round. Intelligent, can be trained for war.<br>
Long memories and short tempers. War elephants carry howdahs and trample anything in their path.

## Elf
**HD 1 | AC 14 | HP 4** | Darkvision | Immune: ghoul paralysis<br>
Atk sword (d8) or longbow (d8) | Mv Normal | Ml 8 | XP 10<br>
Detect secret doors. May cast one spell per day.<br>
Long-lived and fey-touched. Ancient and proud.

## Ettin
**HD 10 | AC 16 | HP 40** | Darkvision<br>
Atk 2 clubs (3d6 each) | Mv Normal | Ml 9 | XP 1000<br>
Two heads—never surprised. Can attack two different targets.<br>
Stinking, dim-witted two-headed giants. Argue with themselves constantly.
