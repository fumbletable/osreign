# Monsters O

---

## Octopus, Giant
**HD 8 | AC 12 | HP 32** | Blindsight<br>
Atk 8 tentacles (d4), bite (d6) | Mv Swim Fast | Ml 7 | XP 640<br>
Constrict: Each tentacle grabs on hit (DC 12 STR to escape). Ink Cloud: 50' radius obscures vision. Jet: Triple speed for 1 round.<br>
Ambush predator lurking in underwater caves. Intelligent enough to use tools.

---

## Ochre Jelly
**HD 5 | AC 11 | HP 20** | Blindsight<br>
Atk pseudopod (2d6) | Mv Slow, Climb Slow | Ml 12 | XP 250<br>
Acid dissolves killed victims (no Raise Dead). Lightning splits it into two jellies with half HP each.<br>
Amorphous amber ooze. Seeps through cracks hunting organic matter.

## Ogre
**HD 4 | AC 10 | HP 16** | Darkvision<br>
Atk greatclub (d10) or spear (d10, thrown Far) | Mv Fast | Ml 9 | XP 160<br>
Brutal Swing: on natural 18+, hits all adjacent creatures.<br>
Stupid and cruel. Eats anything it can catch—including you.

## Ogre Mage
**HD 5 | AC 15 | HP 20** | Darkvision<br>
Atk sword (d12) | Mv Fast | Ml 9 | XP 250<br>
Can: Fly, Invisibility, Darkness (15' radius), human form, Sleep, Charm Person (1/day each). Cone of Frost (DC 12 DEX or 8d6 cold, Near).<br>
Ogre with magical power from Japanese legend. Cunning and cruel.
