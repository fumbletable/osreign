# Homebrew

Community-created content for OSWR 2e. All submissions are reviewed for compatibility before publishing.

**Want to submit your own creation?** Post it in the appropriate homebrew channel on [Discord](https://discord.gg/XpRqQFs3CX). Include the full statblock and a brief description.

---

## What's Here

- [**Monsters**](monsters/) - Community-created creatures → `#homebrew-bestiary`
- [**Ancestries**](ancestries/) - New player ancestries → `#homebrew-ancestry`
- [**Feats**](feats/) - Character options → `#homebrew-feats`
- [**Other**](other/) - Spells, items, and more → `#homebrew-other`

---

## Submission Guidelines

1. **Use the official format** - Follow the statblock style from the core rules
2. **Balance check** - Compare against similar official content
3. **Keep it brief** - One paragraph of flavour is enough
4. **Include your Discord name** - So we can credit you
