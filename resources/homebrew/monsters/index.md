# Homebrew Bestiary

Creatures created by the OSWR community. Each entry credits its creator.

## Allip
*Created by knighterrant1663*

**HD 3 | AC 12 | HP 12** | Incorporeal | Immune: non-magical weapons<br>
Atk touch of insanity (special) | Mv Normal | Ml 12 | XP 90<br>
Touch of Insanity: Drains 1 WIS. Victim at WIS −5 becomes an allip. WIS returns after 90 minutes.<br>
Aura of Babbling Madness: Creatures within 30 ft DC 8 WIS or confused as per spell. Those who save are immune for 24 hours.<br>
Misty apparitions said to be remnants of people driven to insanity and suicide. Some sages theorize they are related to Shadows.
